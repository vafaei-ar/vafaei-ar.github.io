About Me

Accomplished data scientist with a Ph.D. in Physics, specializing in cutting-edge machine learning techniques for health-tech and other industries. Expert in federated learning, sustainable AI models, and deep learning applications in healthcare. Passionate about leveraging AI to solve real-world problems and advancing AI sustainability and trustworthiness.

Federated Learning Data Imputation AI Sustainability Language Models Deep Learning Digital Pathology Radio Astronomy Anomaly Detection Medical Imaging Cosmological Parameter Estimation

Key Projects

  • Federated Learning Framework: Developed a system for distributed machine learning across healthcare institutions, preserving data privacy.
  • AI Sustainability Toolkit: Created tools for measuring and optimizing AI models' environmental impact, focusing on energy efficiency.
  • Medical Image Analysis: Implemented deep learning models for digital pathology, including tissue classification and anomaly detection.
  • Radio Astronomy Data Processing: Contributed to the HIRAX project with algorithms for cosmological parameter estimation and interference mitigation.
  • SKA Source Detection: Developed machine learning techniques for identifying astronomical sources in radio telescope data.
  • EHR Data Imputation: Designed algorithms for imputing missing data in electronic health records.
  • ECG Analysis: Contributed to an AI-assisted decision support system for ECG interpretation.
